About Us

Kafeteria Kolej Siswa Abdul Rahman Limbong or more well known as Limbong is a cafeteria that consists of more than 10 food booth that offers lots of food variety. It is student-centered cafeteria as it is located within Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT). We design a system that will be allow students, staffs or anyone who comes to eat at Limbong to order the food served by the boothes just at the tip of the finger.
